about us

Best Student Living

Assisco solutions is a company which is based in Luton. We are here to give advice, and services for international students who come to study at The University Of Bedfordshire.

Assisco solutions was founded in 2012 by a group of students who wanted to start helping international students in need. in 2013, the group was doing very well and decided it would be more beneficial if they cretaed a website instead. the website was up and running within six months. it strted off a bit slow, but within a month or so it started picking up quite drastically. the services we have on here are quite simple and straight forward, to make it easier to use for those who need it.

we do our best to help students through are website and if a meeting is required, then a memeber of the team is happy to have a meeting with the student to see if they can be of more help.
